
# 防蠢SOP文檔 | Anti-Stupid SOP Docs

## 理念 | Philosophy
這是一套在極端壓力下淬煉而成的「5分鐘防蠢決策框架」，用4步驟幫你堵住90%的低級錯誤。
This is a "5-Minute Anti-Stupidity Decision Framework" forged under extreme pressure, helping you block 90% of basic mistakes with 4 steps.

## 核心價值 | Core Value
- 不依賴智商，只依賴流程
- 5分鐘就能完成一次完整檢查
- 適用職場、創業、生活所有決策場景
- 與AI協作生成，可持續迭代優化

- No reliance on IQ, only on process
- Complete a full check in 5 minutes
- Applicable to all decision scenarios: work, startups, life
- Co-created with AI, continuously iterable and optimizable

## 完整使用流程 | Full Workflow
1.  **Parrot Mode（對齊理解）**：用自己的話重述需求，確認模糊詞彙
2.  **Three-Path Planning（多路徑備選）**：準備保守、標準、突破三種方案
3.  **Devil’s Advocate（紅隊檢驗）**：自我質疑，找出最弱環節與備案
4.  **Layperson Validation（價值翻譯）**：用三句話向門外漢解釋你的計畫

## 常見問題 | FAQ
Q: 為什麼要做「Parrot Mode」？
A: 避免「聽懂了」但其實完全理解錯的悲劇，是所有錯誤的源頭。
Q: Why "Parrot Mode"?
A: To avoid the tragedy of "I understand" but actually misunderstanding completely, which is the source of all mistakes.

Q: 一定要準備三個路徑嗎？
A: 強迫你跳出「只有一種選擇」的思維盲區，避免孤注一擲。
Q: Must I prepare three paths?
A: To force you out of the "only one option" mindset blind spot and avoid putting all eggs in one basket.
